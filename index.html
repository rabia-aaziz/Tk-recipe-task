Chippy Blonde Brownie

Recipe by Chef Shireen Anwar

Ingredients

  • Self raising flour 1 ½ cup
  • Salt ½ tsp
  • Butter 8 ozs
  • Brown sugar 2 cups
  • Eggs 4
  • Vanilla essence 1 tsp
  • Chocolate chips ½ + ½ cup
  • Pecans/ walnuts chopped ½ cup

Cooking Directions

  1. Sift self rising flour and salt. Keep aside.
  2. Beat butter and sugar till light and fluffy, add eggs one by one.
  3. Add vanilla essence then fold in self rising flour.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans, pour in a grease pan, and bake in pre heated oven at 180 for 25 to 30 minutes.
  5. Cut into slices and serve.
